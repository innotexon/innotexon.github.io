## Projects Overview

Below is a summary of some of the key projects being worked on at **INNOTEXON**:

### **Quantum-Tunneled Energy Bridge (QTEB)**

QTEB is a groundbreaking project that integrates quantum tunneling with black hole energy harvesting to create a new form of energy transfer. This system allows for efficient and nearly limitless energy transmission over vast distances, opening doors to revolutionary energy solutions for industries and space exploration.

For more information, visit [QTEB Project Page](https://innotexon.com).

### **Hand of God (HOG)**

HOG is a machine that can manipulate atoms at a subatomic level, allowing for the creation of new materials and structures that are beyond the reach of traditional molecular engineering. This system could be used to produce everything from complex biological molecules to advanced forms of exotic matter.

Learn more about HOG by visiting [HOG Project Page](https://innotexon.com).

### **BioBalancer (BB)**

The BioBalancer system is designed to be an active agent in the fight against climate change. It captures harmful gases such as CO2 and methane from the atmosphere and converts them into harmless or useful byproducts like oxygen, helping to reduce the impact of pollution and contribute to cleaner air and healthier ecosystems.

Explore the BioBalancer project at [BB Project Page](https://innotexon.com).
